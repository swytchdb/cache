# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Current and last major versions only.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities by email to [security@getswytch.com](mailto:security@getswytch.com).

To help us triage the report, please include:
- the affected product, component, and version(s)
- detailed reproduction steps or a proof of concept
- the potential impact and any observed behavior
- relevant logs, screenshots, or other supporting material

Encrypted reporting is not currently documented with a public PGP key in this repository. If you need to share sensitive details securely, contact us at the address above and request coordination for secure transmission.

We will aim to acknowledge receipt within 3 business days and provide a follow-up status update after initial triage.
